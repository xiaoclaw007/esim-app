📱 How to Install Your eSIM

  • 1

    Open Settings

    iPhone: Settings → Cellular → Add eSIM
    Android: Settings → Network & Internet → SIMs → Add SIM

  • 2

    Scan the QR Code

    Use your phone's camera to scan the QR code above, or enter the details manually.

  • 3

    Label Your eSIM

    Give it a recognizable name like "Japan Travel" so you can identify it easily.

  • 4

    Enable Data Roaming

    Go to Cellular → select your eSIM → turn on Data Roaming. This is required for the eSIM to work abroad.

  • 5

    You're Connected! 🎉

    Your eSIM will activate automatically when you arrive in Japan. Enjoy your trip!
